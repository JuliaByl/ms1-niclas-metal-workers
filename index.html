Who are we?

We are a team of experienced metalworkers from North Dublin, who have been in the game for over 20 years. We work with anything metal- and roof related. Niclas started the company in 2002 and it has since then grown and become a well known place in the surrounding area. The team is capable of working both bigger projects as well as smaller, fine detailed ones. Contact us to discuss your projects!


What locations do we cover?

We mainly do work on the north side of dublin, but we serve the entire county. Depending on the project, we can travel even further. For example, last year our skills were wanted on a bigger building project in Galway, and we traveled there to work on it for a few days, before going back to our base workshop.

What do we specialize in?

While we do have our favourite types of projects, we love to do all kinds of different work. Our vast area of expertise makes us suitable for all your needs. With that said, one of the areas we pride ourselves in a bit extra is churches. Many of the bigger churches around county Dublin that you see will have had at least something done by us. We love to do the small detailed projects, and work closely with clients to deliver custom works in a timely manner.
